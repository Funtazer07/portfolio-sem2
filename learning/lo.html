learning outcome 1: Interactive media products

Student orients in the relevant tech, media and design landscape and creates interactive media products that has tested with users and stakeholders.

learning outcome 2: Development & version control

Student explores front-end development languages, writes code and documents in a version control environment.

learning outcome 3: Iterative design

Student explores and uses professional design tools and iteratively design visual works.

learning outcome 4: Professional standard

Student applies professional practice, both individually and in teams, in the areas of project organisation, communication with stakeholders, exploratory research, and reporting.

learning outcome 5: Personal Leadership

Student takes the initiative in asking for, and reflecting on, feedback. Student identifies his own core values as the basis for his study career and professional development.
